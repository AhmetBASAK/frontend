DEVELOPMENT

Whether you need specialists in research, new development, prototyping, modification, reuse, re-engineering, maintenance, or any other activities that result in software products, "IT Talent Turkey" can deliver the right talent for you. DEV OPS

If you're looking to evolve and improve your product at a faster pace than simply using traditional software development and infrastructure management processes, "IT Talent Turkey" can provide  DevOps specialists with the ability to deliver applications and services at a high velocity. By combing cultural philosophies, practices and tools, our DevOps talent increase trust, deliver faster software releases and resolve critical issues quickly, resulting in better management of  both scheduled and unplanned work.

SAP/ERP

"IT Talent Turkey" are experts in SAP/ERP recruitment. With over a decade of experience in identifying freelance specialists, we pride ourselves on the ability to find all modules across the SAP suite and ERP technologies.

SECURITY

"IT Talent Turkey" delivers experts in Information Security, ERP Security and Cyber Security, helping to secure the confidentiality, integrity and availability of your businesses' system data. Our specialists draw from a wealth of experience to safeguard your company against malicious threats, protecting your valuable data and assets.

BIG DATA

We provide experts in handling and studying big data sets that are too complex for traditional data-processing application software. Our Story

IT Talent Turkey was founded with one goal: earn the reputation of being the best IT staffing company in the country by delivering on our promise to only carefully introduce the right candidates for each job.

The IT industry in recent years has been simply developing rapidly. Moreover, according to experts, this industry will continue to develop actively in the coming years. That is why highly qualified specialists in this field are simply worth their weight in gold. Competent and high-quality IT recruiting will be the key to the true success for any company. Our company ITTalentTurkeywill perform a high-quality selection of IT specialists in the shortest possible time and will fully satisfy all your requirements and requests.

ITTalentTurkey will successfully recruit IT staff for companies in Europe, Turkey, Kuwait or Dubai. We will do a search for the employees your company needs in all cities in Turkey and find Rockstart Candidates.

John previously co-founded Employment Trends, a successful and award-winning staffing company named the #1 Best Small Company to Work for in Oregon and landing on Inc’s 500 Fastest Growing Companies list, which eventually merged with a larger company. Following his departure from Employment Trends, John was quickly ready for his next adventure and set out to build a company that combined his extensive experience in both technology and staffing. Before starting ProFocus, John met with several hiring managers who use IT staffing services. They all had the same complaint – the current IT staffing suppliers were sending too many candidates and most of them were inappropriate for the position. ProFocus was built on a model of quality and integrity specifically designed to solve that problem for customers and candidates. Our business model is focused on only making careful introductions of the right candidates. We do this by fully understanding technology, our candidates, and the needs for each position. Hiring managers, candidates, and internal team members have been all-in ever since. They value the high-quality, personal approach we take with each of our candidates and clients and it shows. ProFocus is today one of the fastest-growing private companies in Oregon, appears on the Inc. 500 list and has been recognized by Inavero’s Best of Staffing Awards for both Client and Talent satisfaction.
